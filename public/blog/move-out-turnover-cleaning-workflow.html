Move-out turnover cleaning workflow for landlords

Faster turnovers come from sequencing, not just speed. A clear cleaning workflow reduces rework and helps units reach showing condition sooner.

1) Scope check before scheduling

Confirm carpet-only vs full-unit scope and note odor/stain hotspots. Early scope clarity prevents same-day change orders that delay handoff.

2) Service order matters

In most units, dust/debris removal and hard-surface cleaning should precede final carpet extraction, so carpets stay cleaner before listing photos.

3) Add documentation

Keep simple completion notes and receipts tied to unit number and date. This helps owner reporting and smooths security deposit conversations.
